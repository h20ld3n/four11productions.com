Burn It All Down

Kirt Rufus Hardcastle (1949 - 2018)

Kirt was a Veteran and medic in Vietnam. He had some very strong feelings about that. Kirt was a self-taught philosopher and painter. He worked as an artist for over thirty years and was inspired by Claude Monet. He was drawn to the decay of old sitting next to shiny new. The more that things change, the more they stay the same.
